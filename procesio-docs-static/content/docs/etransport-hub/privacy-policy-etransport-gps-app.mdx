# Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 2024-09-18

Last Updated: 2024-09-18

## 1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how PROCESIO \[Ringhel Team SRL] ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, discloses, and protects your information when you use our application ("App"), which is used by drivers to transport parcels between an origin and a destination. This policy is in compliance with Ordonanța de urgență nr. 115/2023 and Google Play's User Data Policy.

## 2. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

- Location Data: We collect real-time location data from your device to track parcels from origin to destination. This data is collected continuously in the background to comply with legal requirements.
- Personal Data: Information such as your name, contact details, and vehicle information may be collected when you register or use our App.
- Usage Data: We collect data about how you interact with our App, including logs, device information, and app usage statistics.

## 3. Background Location Collection and Usage

Our app requires continuous access to your location in the background to:

- Track and verify the movement of parcels in real-time as mandated by Romanian law (Ordonanța de urgență nr. 115/2023)
- Ensure continuous monitoring of transport routes between pickup and delivery points
- Provide accurate location data to comply with legal transport verification requirements
- Enable route optimization and delivery confirmation features
- Background location access continues even when the app is not in active use
- You can revoke location permissions at any time through your device settings, however this will prevent the app from functioning as required by transport regulations
- Location data is collected only during active transport assignments and is encrypted during transmission and storage

*Background location data is essential for fulfilling the purpose of the application, particularly ensuring compliance with legal requirements. The app enables users to transmit GPS data in real time to meet the mandates of eTransport legislation in Romania. This functionality provides users with a reliable means of adhering to transport regulations while benefiting from accurate parcel tracking and route management.*

## 4. How We Use Your Information

- To track the parcels' movement between origin and destination.
- To comply with legal obligations, including Romanian law Ordonanța de urgență nr. 115/2023.
- To improve and personalize your experience with the App.
- To communicate with you regarding updates, security alerts, and other relevant information.

## 5. Sharing Your Information

We do not share your personal data with third parties except in the following circumstances:

- Legal Compliance: To comply with applicable laws, regulations, or legal requests.
- Service Providers: With trusted service providers who help us operate the App, subject to strict data protection obligations.
- Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred to the new entity.

## 6. Data Retention and Deletion

We retain your data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this policy or as required by law. You may request deletion of your data by contacting us at privacy\@procesio.com.

## 7. Security of Your Information

We take appropriate measures to secure your data from unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse. However, please be aware that no security measures are perfect, and we cannot guarantee the absolute security of your data.

## 8. Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal information. For any requests or inquiries, please contact us at privacy\@procesio.com.

## 9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.

## 10. Explicit Consent

*We will request your explicit consent before accessing your location data for the first time. You can adjust permissions at any time through your device settings. Additionally, the app includes a&#x20;****Start Sending Location Data****&#x20;button and a&#x20;****Stop Sending Location Data****&#x20;button to give you control over when GPS data is transmitted. Location data is only collected and transmitted while the&#x20;****Start Sending Location Data****&#x20;button is active.*

## 11. Children's Data

*Our app is not intended for users under the age of 16, and we do not knowingly collect data from children. If we become aware of any data collected from a child under 16, we will promptly delete it.*
