## **What is a workspace?**

Every account offers a workspace where users store their processes and credentials. Company accounts have the ability to share their workspace with other users in their organization.

While the community plans, as well as the business plan, only grants access to a single workspace, the enterprise plan gives access to multi workspace functionality. This means that accounts having an enterprise plan can invite users to join their workspace and share their resources within a specific workspace. By having multiple workspaces, companies can provision resources per workspace according to needs. 

The owner of the enterprise plan has full administrative rights, including user management, and can add and remove users to the workspace according to their projects' needs.

## **User Management page**

All users added to a workspace will have access to the "User Management" page and will be able to see other members and their roles and status in the workspace. All users part of the workspace will have access to the “User Management” page. This page will provide information on users' names, roles, and status. 

There are two types of workspace members: Admin and Member. Only Admins can add/remove members.

<Callout type="info">
    One company workspace can have more than one admin.
</Callout>

![](/images/docs/GHZ6QyaudlkxUPgJdKf1h_workspaces.png)

### **Adding a user to a workspace**

As a workspace admin, you can invite people to your workspace from "Settings" > "User Management".  Enter the person's email address and click the **Invite** button.

![](/images/docs/Rir35zrRl92nR_iy38nMS_settings.png)

The user is added as a member with the "**Pending invitation**" status. Once the user accepts the invitation to join the workspace, the status changes to "**Active**".

**Cancel invitations**

As a workspace admin, you can cancel an invitation while it is still pending by clicking the Delete icon.



![](/images/docs/rud9TfSsN2aejO5yYALB6_um.png)

<Callout type="info">
The person whose invitation you cancel will still have their invitation email, but the link will not lead them to your Workspace. Instead, they will see the "**You have not been added to the workspace because your invitation has been revoked**" message.
</Callout>

### **Remove members**

To remove users from your workspace, click on the **Delete** icon next to the member's name.

## **Switching between workspaces**

If you have access to several workspaces, you will have the option to see what workspaces you have access to by clicking the dropdown in the upper left corner. You can navigate from one workspace to another by clicking them.&#x20;

![](/images/docs/vAdGgcTANJWdkmp78TLEm_switch.png)

By default, when you log into PROCESIO, you will be logged in your individual account. To switch to another workspace, simply select a different account in the list, no need to log out.
