Now here is a breakdown of what you have to do:

1. Log into PROCESIO
2. Go to your personal workspace
3. Go to Process Designer, click the "Create New" Button and the blank canvas will open (canvas is the white surface where your work with actions to create process flows)
4. Name your process (replace Untitled process. You can try "Course 1")
5. From Platform Action Toolbar, Numerical Folder, drag and drop the **Add operation** on Canvas
6. From Platform Action Toolbar, Numerical Folder, drag and drop the **Subtract operation** on Canvas
7. From Platform Action Toolbar, drag and drop the action named **CallSubprocess**
8. Connect the action as shown in the image above, by dragging and dropping the connecting arrows.
